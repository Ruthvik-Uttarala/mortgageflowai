# Product Research

MortgageFlowAI is an interview project by Ruthvik Uttarala, inspired by public product themes around AngelAi, Celligence, and Sun West Mortgage. It is not affiliated with those companies.

## Publicly Observed Strengths

- Fast 24/7 mortgage assistant.
- Loan officer productivity.
- Realtor and borrower usefulness.
- Mortgage question answering.
- Pending item support.
- Trust and confidence.
- Fair lending and faster workflows.

## Publicly Observed Or Inferred Problems

- Verification code and login friction.
- Deep link and in-app navigation friction.
- Need for clearer task routing.
- Need for emotional guidance during stressful borrower moments.
- Need for explainable decision paths in financial AI.
- Need for human escalation when AI confidence is low.

## Product Translation

MortgageFlowAI preserves what users publicly appear to value: speed, 24/7 mortgage confidence, pending-item visibility, and professional productivity. It adds product depth in areas that matter for financial workflows: verification recovery, deep link task routing, fair lending consistency, deterministic explanations, audit trails, and human escalation when confidence is low.
