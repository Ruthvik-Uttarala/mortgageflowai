Use this file to track the core flows required by the interview project.

- Borrower task flow: open `/`, review loan status, missing documents, and next best action.
- Loan officer scenario flow: submit the scenario form and confirm the explainable result changes.
- Verification recovery flow: open `/tasks/verification-recovery` and confirm recovery copy is direct and human friendly.
- Deep link routing flow: open `/tasks/loan-status`, `/tasks/missing-document`, `/tasks/condition-review`, and `/tasks/scenario-answer`.
